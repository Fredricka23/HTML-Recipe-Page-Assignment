Fresh Wild Spinach

Wild fresh spinach is a and tasty healthy and tasty meal. It can be served as a side dish or a quick meal. It is packed with natural vitamins, it is a meal that makes you feel energized and satisfied.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 7 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 600g fresh spinach leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 white onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash and drain the spinach leaves thoroughly.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add white onion and cook until it smells good (about 30 seconds).
  4. Add spinach to the pan and cook until wilted, stir it occasionally (about 2-3 minutes).
  5. Season with salt, black pepper.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your meal!

Tips

For an extra flavor, sprinkle dried crushed chilli on top before serving.
